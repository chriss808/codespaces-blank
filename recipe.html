how to make parfaits

A parfait is classified as a sweet but simple dessert that mainly consists of yogurt and the fruit/toppings of your choice to make it sweet and savory!

  • yogurt
  • rasberries
  • blueberries
  1. grab a cup
  2. fill 1/4 of the cup with some yogurt
  3. layer some fruit on top of the yogurt to create an even layer
  4. repeat the 2nd and 3rd steps until your cup is full
  5. add some final fruits/toppings of your choice and voila!
